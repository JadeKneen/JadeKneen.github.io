Welcome to Jade's Portfolio

Software Engineer with a passion for UX

Hi there, I'm glad you wanted to check out my portfolio!

First, let me introduce myself:

  • My name is Jade
  • I've been coding since April 2019
  • I'm a dog person

Now that that's out of the way, let's get down to business.

Despite having an interest in tech since I was young, I would have never imagined becoming a coder.

I wasn't breaking electronics apart before I could crawl, MySpace never even made it to the Isle of Man so I never built up my coding skills there, and DreamWeaver was something I only got to dabble with in the IT rooms at lunch time.

Then I got a call from a coding bootcamp in Manchester offering me a three week introductory course to coding and I thought "what do I have to lose?"

Well, here we are. A three week beginner's course, a three month bootcamp and a few exams later I started to get some work under my belt. Check those out further down!
